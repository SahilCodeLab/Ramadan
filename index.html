Namaz Ke Baad Ki Dua

          Rabbana Atina Fid Dunya'Hasanatanw-wa Fil Aakhirati,Hasanatanw-wa-Qina 'Azaaban-Naar


Ya Allah!  
Hum tere gunahgaar bande hain,  
Teri rehmat ke mohtaj hain,  
Hum se jo bhi ghaltiyan ho gayi,  
Jo bhi gunah anjaane me ya jaane me ho gaye,  
Ya Rabb, tu sab maaf farma de.  

Ya Allah!  
Tera karam na hota toh hum kahaan jaate?  
Teri rehmat na hoti toh hum mitt jaate!  
Bas hum par apni rehmat barsa de,  
Humein hamesha apni hifazat me rakh.  

Ya Allah!  
Humein behtareen zindagi ata farma,  
Humein halal rizq de,  
Humein imaan ki roshni ata farma,  
Aur humein akhirat me jannat-ul-firdous ata farma.  

Ya Allah!  
Hamare dil ke dukh dho de,  
Hamare gham door kar de,  
Jo bhi musibat hai, jo bhi pareshani hai,  
Ya Allah, tu sab door kar de!  

Ya Allah!  
Humein kabhi akela mat chhodna,  
Hamare dil ki har fariyad sun lena,  
Hamare gunahon ko maaf farma dena.  

Ya Allah! Hum Tere pyaare Habib ﷺ ka wasila dete hain,  
Humein har balaa se mehfooz farma,  
Humein duniya aur aakhirat ki behtareen nematein ata farma.  

### Dua for Palestine & Oppressed Muslims  

Ya Allah!  
Palestine ke mazloom bhai-behnon par apna karam farma,  
Unke dukh door kar de,  
Unke zakhm bhar de,  
Unki madad farma, unko sabr aur himmat de.  

Ya Allah!  
Jo bachay bina maa-baap ke ho gaye,  
Jo maaen apne laal kho chuki hain,  
Jo ghar barbad ho chuke hain,  
Un sabko apni rehmat me le le,  
Aur zalimon se inka inteqam le!  

Ya Allah!  
Har mazloom ko insaaf ata farma,  
Unhe quwwat de, unko madad farma.  
Unke dukh, unke gham door kar,  
Aur unko jannat-ul-firdous ka waasta dekar sabr de.  

Ya Allah!  
Duniya ke har mazloom ke liye,  
Jo zulm sah rahe hain, jo takleef me hain,  
Unke liye aasani paida farma,  
Unki madad farma, unke dukh door farma.  

Ya Rabb!  
Hamari be-hisiyat aur be-amali ko maaf kar,  
Humein behtar insan bana,  
Humein sirat-ul-mustaqeem pe chala,  
Aur humein duniya aur aakhirat dono me kamiyabi de.  

Ameen Ya Rabbal ‘Alamin.
